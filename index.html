Background: Happy Subtitle Channel was founded in 2021 to provide high-quality subtitles and localization for long-form and episodic video content, helping creators and platforms reach wider, multilingual audiences.

Challenge: Scaling subtitle production while maintaining quality, consistency, and turnaround time across a large volume of episodes.

Actions:

  • Designed and managed end-to-end subtitle workflows, from transcription and translation to QA and publishing.

  • Managed scheduling and delivery to meet tight publishing timelines.

Results:

  • Successfully delivered subtitles for 17,000+ episodes since 2021.
  • Improved turnaround speed while maintaining consistent quality across content.
  • Enabled creators and platforms to expand reach and engagement through multilingual accessibility.
